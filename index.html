The Evolution of Music

Attributes:

Acousticness

The higher the value, the more acoustic the song.

Danceability

The higher the value, the easier it is to dance to this song.

Energy

The higher the value, the more energetic the song.

Liveness

The more digitally produced a song is, the lower the value.

Speechiness

The higher the value, the more the song is based on speech.

Valence

The higher the value, the more positive the mood.

Tempo

The tempo of the song.

Duration

The duration of the song.

About:

Check the boxes on the left to dispay the attributes on the chart
Hover the circles to see what song they represent. Click the circle to listen to the song.
The goal of this project was to identify trends in music over the last 50+ years
The Data for this project was taken from a dataset of 914,000 rows containing all albums and tracks in the Billboard 200 from 1/5/1963 to 1/19/2019.
This dataset was too large for a browser to load so the dataset was condensed into the top 10 songs of every week in the original period of time.
