About

The dataset includes every album that was on the Billboard 200 from 1/5/1963 to 1/19/2019. The goal of this project was to identify trends in the Music Industry over the last 50+ years. To help facilitate this goal, historical images have been overlaid below the x-axis corresponding to each year. Each year has that year’s Time Magazine Person of the Year cover as well as the cover of that year’s Album of the Year.



Attributes are color-coded between the graph and the checkboxes.



Hover over each attribute to see an explanation and the track with the highest rating in each attribute.



Each attribute was gathered on a different scale so 0 = the lowest point and 100 = the highest point of each attribute.
