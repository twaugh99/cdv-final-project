Attributes:

Acousticness

The higher the value, the more acoustic the song is.

Danceability

The higher the value, the easier it is to dance to this song.

Energy

The higher the value, the more energetic the song is.

Liveness

The more digitally produced a song is, the lower the value.

Speechiness

The higher the value, the more the song is based on speech.

Valence

The higher the value, the more positive the mood.

Tempo

The tempo of the song.

Duration

The duration of the song.





About:

The goal of this project was to identify trends in music over the last 50+ years

The Data for this project was taken from a dataset of 914,000 rows containing all albums and tracks in the Billboard 200 from 1/5/1963 to 1/19/2019. Here is a link to the dataset. This dataset was too large for a browser to load so the dataset was condensed into the top 10 songs of every week in the original period of time.
